Lebanese Cuisine

Mezze Experience

Lebanese dining is all about sharing! Try dozens of small dishes like creamy hummus, refreshing tabbouleh, crispy falafel, and stuffed grape leaves. Every meal is a feast for the senses.

Street Food Adventures

Grab a manakish (flatbread with zaatar or cheese) for breakfast, enjoy a shawarma wrap for lunch, and end your day with knefeh - a sweet cheese pastry soaked in syrup. Street food in Lebanon is absolutely delicious!

Sweet Treats

Lebanese desserts are legendary! Try baklava with its layers of phyllo and honey, creamy ashta ice cream, or date-filled maamoul cookies. Don't forget to pair them with strong Arabic coffee.

Grilled Meats

Experience the art of Lebanese grilling with tender kebabs, juicy chicken tawook, and flavorful kafta. These dishes are often served with garlic sauce and grilled vegetables.

Fresh Salads

Enjoy vibrant salads like fattoush with crispy pita chips and sumac, or refreshing tabbouleh made with bulgur, parsley, tomatoes, and lemon juice.

Traditional Stews

Warm up with hearty Lebanese stews like molokhia (jute leaves), fasoulia (white beans in tomato sauce), or bamia (okra stew). Perfect comfort food!
