Must-Visit Historic Sites

Baalbek

Home to some of the largest Roman temples ever built. The Temple of Bacchus is incredibly well-preserved and will transport you back 2,000 years. Walking through these massive columns is like stepping into a time machine!

Byblos (Jbeil)

One of the oldest continuously inhabited cities on Earth, dating back over 7,000 years. Explore the medieval castle, walk through ancient souks, and enjoy fresh seafood by the historic harbor.

Jeita Grotto

A breathtaking natural wonder featuring two separate caves filled with stunning rock formations. Take a boat ride through the lower cave's underground river and marvel at nature's artwork created over millions of years.

Anjar

Discover the ruins of an ancient Umayyad city with well-preserved streets and structures. This archaeological site offers a glimpse into early Islamic urban planning and architecture.
