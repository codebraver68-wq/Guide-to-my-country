Outdoor Adventures

Cedar Forests

Visit the ancient cedar trees that have stood for thousands of years. These majestic trees are Lebanon's national symbol and are mentioned in ancient texts. The forest air is fresh and peaceful.

Mountain Skiing

Lebanon is unique - you can ski in the mountains in the morning and swim in the Mediterranean by afternoon! The ski resorts offer great slopes and stunning mountain views.

Mediterranean Beaches

Relax on beautiful beaches along the coast. The water is crystal clear and perfect for swimming. Many beach clubs offer water sports, good food, and a fun atmosphere.

Hiking Trails

Trek the Lebanon Mountain Trail, a 470km path that crosses the country from north to south. Experience diverse landscapes, villages, and stunning viewpoints.
