Discover Lebanon

Welcome to Lebanon!

Lebanon is a beautiful Mediterranean country where ancient history comes alive. From Roman temples to cedar forests, from bustling markets to stunning beaches, Lebanon is full of surprises. Despite being small in size, it offers incredible diversity in landscapes, cultures, and experiences that will make your visit unforgettable.

Helpful Travel Tips

  • Best seasons to visit are spring (March-May) with blooming flowers and fall (September-November) with perfect weather
  • Arabic is the main language, but most people speak English and French too, so communication is easy
  • Lebanese hospitality is famous - locals love to share their culture and will often invite you for coffee or meals
  • Try to visit different regions - the mountains, coast, and valleys each have their own character
  • Don't leave without trying every dessert you can find - Lebanese sweets are truly special!
